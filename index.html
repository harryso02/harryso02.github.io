Biography

Harry is a tech enthusiast with a profound interest in cognitive science and social psychology. He is currently pursuing his Bachelor degree in Psychology at The Chinese University of Hong Kong and has been awarded Dean's List for 2 consecutive years.

Harry has worked as a part-time online coach at Jockey Club TourHeart+ Project, which is an online platform providing guided self-help low intensity psychological intervention. He is supervised by Prof. Winnie Wing-sze, MAK, two clinical psychologists, and a team of psychological wellbeing officers, and has coached working adults participated in the online courses delivering rumination-based cognitive-behavioral therapy (RFCBT), mindfulness-based therapy (MBT), and transdiagnostic cognitive-behavioral therapy (TCBT).

Harry has also interned in Expivotal Consultancy co-founded by a group of organizational psychologists. His contributions include developing mock assessment center tasks, refining the personality-based competency assessment scale, publishing instagram posts and online free resources, and technical support such as conducting search engine optimization (SEO) and improving user experience (UX) of the company website.
